Security Audit

Security is of the utmost importance to SQL on Call. Our clients do not want to have to send out a letter to their customers stating their data has been put in jeopardy. SQL on Call will look at your SQL Servers and show you where we believe people can place your company and your customer’s information at risk.

A SQL on Call Security Audit will help you with an audit, by showing that you have policies in place and you have taken corrective action to meet any deficiencies. A SQL on Call expert will provide you with a report that will outline where security changes can be made.

A professional service with a personal touch, our staff will quickly integrate with your team. Our Pro-Active Maintenance client has a Primary DBA and a Secondary DBA assigned to them. With our staff working closely with your staff, we will gain an indepth knowledge of your database and its inner workings. We can make recommendations that will improve your performance, stability and recoverability. Each one of our DBA’s will make sure that we recommend the best solutions for your company. They will evaluate and consider all aspects of your goals including timelines, budgets and requirements.
