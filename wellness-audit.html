Wellness Audit

Just as people go to the doctor for a physical before the football season starts, your SQL Server should have its health checked to make sure that when the big game gets here something does not fail. A failed SQL Server means lost revenue and clients shopping elsewhere.

SQL on Call checks the health of your SQL Server starting from the hardware up. If we see any issues, they will be brought to your attention with recommendations on how to fix the problem. Many times we can make changes after your approval with no additional costs.

A professional service with a personal touch, our staff will quickly integrate with your team. Our Pro-Active Maintenance client has a Primary DBA and a Secondary DBA assigned to them. With our staff working closely with your staff, we will gain an indepth knowledge of your database and its inner workings. We can make recommendations that will improve your performance, stability and recoverability. Each one of our DBA’s will make sure that we recommend the best solutions for your company. They will evaluate and consider all aspects of your goals including timelines, budgets and requirements.
